About CU-Note

This website will allow all coventry university students to keep in contact no matter what course or department you are from.

The hope is that students will be more connected than ever, by using this platform to keep in contact with each other.

There are some ground rules however:

  • Students must use this for relevant information
  • Students need to make sure that when they submit, they note their course and year as to make sure all viewers understand
  • It would be beneficial if submissions were anonymous
